CodePilot Logo Direction

Premium hacker-tool energy: sharp, secretive, capable.

Recommended Direction: Obsidian Pilot Mark
App Icon Mood

Obsidian Pilot Mark

An abstract mark that feels like a terminal prompt discovered a personality. It hints at >_, a narrowed eye, and a directional strike, without turning into a cartoon face.

The silhouette stays clean enough for iOS app icon scale, but the interior glow and asymmetry make it feel like a private tool used by people who know exactly what they are doing.

Shape Language

Rounded-square outer frame for App Icon compatibility, but with subtle cut-corner tension. Inside, a directional prompt stroke and a suspicious, half-lidded bar create personality without literal illustration.

Color Story

Obsidian black, surgical teal-green, and a whisper of cold blue. It should feel expensive, nocturnal, and slightly radioactive rather than playful neon gamer branding.

Personality

Not a mascot. Not a startup gradient blob. More like a covert operator's tool badge: quiet confidence, technical competence, and a tiny smirk.

What this should make you feel

"This app is a sharp little machine. It sees the bridge, knows the tunnel, and can do things regular apps cannot."
